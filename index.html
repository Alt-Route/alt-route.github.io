--- # Feel free to add content and custom Front Matter to this file. # To modify the layout, see https://jekyllrb.com/docs/themes/#overriding-theme-defaults title: home layout: home about_title: What is Alt Route? about_section: Alt route is a fully funded project through a grant received from RisingYouth and TakingITGlobal. The project works to create safe, accessible, and inclusive skatepark experiences for youth with vision loss and low vision. We do this by implementing adaptations to skateparks; adaptations that we've pioneered through hands-on research with our skate team. feature_one_icon: icon-line2-eye feature_one_subtitle: Make the Invisible Visible feature_one_title: High Visibility Markers feature_one_description: We use brightly coloured series of lines and patterns layed out onto the concrete with a "colour code" that helps skaters with low-vision to be able to 'see' transitions, lines and landing spots. feature_two_icon: icon-line2-volume-2 feature_two_subtitle: Hear What's Coming feature_two_title: Auditory Beacons feature_two_description: Audio beacons emit a beeping or ticking noise that allow low vision and no vision skateboarders the ability to determine where skatepark obstacles are and used to provide reference for ramps, stairs, drops, and gaps. feature_three_icon: icon-line2-speedometer feature_three_subtitle: Get a Feel for the Park feature_three_title: Rumble Strips feature_three_description: Just like rumble strips on a road are designed to get your attention. Rumble strips in the skatepark give low vision and no vision skaters warning of the more advanced skateboarding obstacles in a park. walkthrough_bg_img: /assets/images/skate/cass1.jpg walkthrough_thumbnail_img: /assets/images/skate/adaptations-demo.png walkthrough_subtitle: Adaptation Demonstration walkthrough_title: Making A Roll-in & Fly-out Visible walkthrough_description: Alt Route's Matt and Curtis explain some of the visibility markers placed strategically to help Cassandra work towards her goal of 'flying out' of the bathtub bowl at Calgary's Shaw Millenium Park. walkthrough_video_url: https://vimeo.com/576544531 testimonials_bg_img: /assets/images/skate/testimonialsbg.jpg ---
